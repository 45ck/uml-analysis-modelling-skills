# Model Consistency Checklist

- Are all major actors represented in the use-case model?
- Do described flows appear in activity or sequence diagrams?
- Do sequence participants exist in the class model?
- Are multiplicities plausible and consistent with scenarios?
- Do state transitions align with use-case or activity flows?
- Does deployment placement contradict any runtime assumption?
- Are domain models free of premature implementation detail?
- Are design models explicit enough to support implementation?
